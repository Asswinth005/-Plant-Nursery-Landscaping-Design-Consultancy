Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment to visit the nursery?
No appointment needed! Our nursery is open during regular hours. For design consultations, we recommend booking in advance.
Do you offer plant delivery?
Yes, we deliver within 50 miles of Austin. Free delivery on orders over $75.
Can I bring my own plant for diagnosis?
Absolutely! Our horticulturists offer free plant health checks. Just bring a sample or photo.
What payment methods do you accept?
Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, cash, and financing options available for large projects.

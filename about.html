OUR STORY

Rooted in Nature,
Driven by Passion

Founded in 2015, Verdant Essence has grown from a small urban nursery into a full-service landscape architecture studio — without ever losing our love for the soil.

Our Mission

To reconnect people with the natural world through regenerative landscapes, rare plant conservation, and design that honors ecological balance. Every project is a step toward a greener future.

— Cultivate, don't just decorate —

Our Vision

A world where every green space tells a story of biodiversity, resilience, and beauty. We envision cities where native plants thrive, and every person has access to expert botanical guidance.

— Nature first, always —
OUR JOURNEY

From Seed to Canopy

A decade of growth, learning, and cultivating green sanctuaries.

2015Humble Beginnings

Opened as a boutique nursery in Austin with 200 plant species. First landscape design client: a 200 sqft urban balcony.

2018Studio Expansion

Launched full landscape architecture division. Completed first estate project (3 acres) featuring native prairie restoration.

2021Sustainability Award

Received Texas Excellence in Ecological Design award. Opened second nursery location focused on drought-tolerant natives.

2025Global Reach

1,200+ projects completed, 50+ team members, and a thriving community of 10k+ plant enthusiasts. New chapter: international consultations.
